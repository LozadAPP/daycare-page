Our Services

Comprehensive childcare solutions tailored for your family's needs

Early Childhood Education

At Butterfly Home Child Care, we offer a structured early education program that nurtures cognitive, language, and emotional development in a loving environment.

Program highlights: Literacy foundations through storytelling, early math skills with sensory activities, exploration of basic science concepts, and hands-on creative arts.

Our curriculum is age-appropriate and designed to foster curiosity, confidence, and a lifelong love for learning.

Creative Development

Creativity is essential for a child's growth. We provide daily opportunities for children to express themselves through art, music, storytelling, and imaginative play.

Creative services include: Sensory-based art projects, music and movement sessions, role-playing activities, and open-ended exploration with a variety of materials.

Every child's work is celebrated to encourage self-expression and build self-esteem.

Physical Wellness and Outdoor Play

Physical activity is a vital part of our daily schedule. Our outdoor and indoor activities promote strength, balance, coordination, and healthy habits.

Offered activities: Outdoor obstacle courses, group games, yoga for children, nature walks, and free play that encourages exploration and social interaction.

Our secure outdoor area allows children to safely enjoy physical activities year-round under staff supervision.

Social and Emotional Development

We support children's emotional intelligence and social skills through structured group activities and positive role modeling.

Key elements include: Group story times, cooperative games, guided peer interactions, emotional recognition exercises, and empathy-building activities.

For our youngest children, we create strong bonds with consistent caregivers to foster trust, resilience, and security.

Extended Care Services

We also provide extended care services, including after-hours and overnight stays, to accommodate your schedule and ensure continuous support for your child.

Nannies and Babysitters

"The best and biggest benefit to hiring a nanny or babysitter is convenience," says Katie Bugbee, senior managing editor and parenting expert for Care.com.

"Daycare teachers can help you as nannies or babysitters on weekends, holidays, vacations, or whenever you need them after daycare hours."

"Depending on where you live, a nanny for two children can often be less expensive than two kids in daycare," says Bugbee.

Questions to Consider

  • ✔️ How often will we need a sitter?
  • ✔️ How flexible do their hours need to be?
  • ✔️ Will we need the sitter to drive the kids?
  • ✔️ What additional tasks might we expect?
  • ✔️ What certifications should they have?
  • ✔️ What energy level fits our family?
  • ✔️ How much experience is required?

Important Considerations

Working with the daycare teachers as a babysitter is a benefit for everyone because it's not difficult for the child to live with the teachers (outside of daycare hours) since they are together all week, and this guarantees a good relationship and a lot of trust.
