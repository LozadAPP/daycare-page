Our Services

We offer a safe and nurturing environment where children can grow, explore, and thrive. Our licensed family childcare in San Rafael is designed to meet the needs of both children and their families.

General Information

  • Location: San Rafael, California
  • License: Family Home Childcare License (State Certified)
  • Languages: English & Spanish
  • Age Group: Accepting children from 6 months to 5 years old
  • Operating Hours: Monday to Friday, 7:30 AM – 5:30 PM

What We Provide

  • Nutritious meals and snacks (included in tuition)
  • Potty training support
  • Daily communication with parents
  • Educational play-based activities
  • Arts & crafts, music, storytelling, and more
  • Safe indoor and outdoor play areas

Why Families Choose Us

At Butterfly Child Care, we treat each child with love and respect, helping them build confidence, independence, and kindness. We provide a "home away from home" where parents can feel at ease knowing their children are well cared for.
