About Us

We are a licensed Family Home Childcare located in San Rafael, dedicated to providing a nurturing environment where children can thrive.

Our mission is to offer a safe and fun space for children to learn, grow, and develop essential life skills that will benefit them throughout their lives.

We care for each child with love, heartfelt education, and patient guidance, helping them reach their full potential while promoting healthy physical and mental development.

Our Program

Through our carefully designed program, children experience new opportunities for exploration and discovery. We focus on all areas of early childhood development including physical, emotional, social and cognitive growth.

Our play-based curriculum includes activities like dramatic play, arts & crafts, music, dance, and reading, all designed to stimulate young minds in a fun, engaging way.

Children flourish in our warm, home-style environment, allowing parents to go to work with complete peace of mind.
