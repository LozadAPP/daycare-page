About Butterfly Home Child Care

We are a licensed Family Home Childcare located in beautiful San Rafael, California. At Butterfly, we create a warm, nurturing environment where children feel safe to explore, learn, and grow. Our dedicated team provides personalized care that supports each child's unique developmental journey.

Our Philosophy

We believe in learning through play, fostering curiosity, and building confidence in a home-like setting.

Safe, clean, and stimulating spaces designed specifically for early childhood development.
