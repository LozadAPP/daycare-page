About Our Daycare

Butterfly Home Child Care is a licensed family daycare located in the heart of Terra Linda, San Rafael. For over 15 years, we've provided a nurturing environment where children can learn, grow, and thrive. Our home-like setting offers the perfect balance of structured learning and creative play.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, loving environment that fosters early childhood development through play-based learning.

Our Approach

Personalized care with a focus on social, emotional, and cognitive development in a bilingual setting.
